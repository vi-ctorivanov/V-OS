@[Jagged Orbit] is a music video for Halftribe & Spinnet's [Jagged Orbit](https://synphaera.bandcamp.com/track/jagged-orbit).

This project was created using photogrammetry of landscape videos taken with a drone, which were then used as force fields for various particle systems.

![Computer-generated image of a collection of pink an teal particles forming 3 floating spheres and a sloped surface under them, in a dark void.](jagged orbit/2.jpg)
