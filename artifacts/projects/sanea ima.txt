I designed and developed my good friend and collaborator [Sanea Ima](https://saneaima.com)'s portfolio website.

The goal was to create a fairly traditional project showcase space with a style that highlights the structure of the site's layout. For that purpose, I opted for a clean construction sketch look with heavy use of full-page dotted lines across major layout elements.

Because Sanea Ima is a composer, I also handcrafted a music player interface for their audio files.

Developed with the help of [GRAV CMS](https://getgrav.org/).
