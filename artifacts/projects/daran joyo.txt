I worked with Daran to realize and implement her portfolio design, check it out [here](https://daranjoyo.com)!

I also helped define the behavior of its more technical elements; the design featured a fun challenge with a sparse grid and hero image which smoothly swapped its photo as the page scrolled.

We also pondered on an interactive background which felt both dynamic yet not too gimmicky. Ultimately, we settled on a subtle and calm multi-point gradient with primitive repulsive forces where the user can push the points around using their mouse.

Developed using [Astro](https://astro.build/) as I've grown to favor static websites.
