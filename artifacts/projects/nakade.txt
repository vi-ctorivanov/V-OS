![](nakade/1.mp4)

@[Nakade] was created for [Synchrony 2020](https://synchrony.nyc/2020/) in an 11-hour train ride from New York to Montreal, winning first place in the Demo category.

The project was created in collaboration with [Léa Demeule](https://leademeule.com/), who took the role of graphics programmer for point cloud rendering, point cloud manipulation, post processing effects, and scene management.

![Computer-generated image of a plaza with trees and concrete flooring made up of a cloud of smeared points in space.](nakade/1.jpg)

All environments in this demo were made using photogrammetry of locations in New York and the train the jam took place in.

![Cloud of smeared points depicting a train interior spiraling around the center of the frame.](nakade/2.jpg)
