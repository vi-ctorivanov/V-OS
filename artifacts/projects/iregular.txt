I worked with the team at Iregular to produce 3 of their 5 interactive artworks in their Our Common Home series. These works leverage facial and body tracking to drive interactive experiences that have been projected on the faces of iconic buildings around the world, including Montreal, Chicago, Dubai, and Los Angeles.

I worked with our art director to execute their ideas on a technical level, producing visual assets and programming interactive behaviors while working with engineers to interface with their tracking technology. And lots (*lots*) of testing and iterating on pieces on-site at various exhibition locations.

# Iregular's Artist Statement

> OUR COMMON HOME is an interactive public art exhibition that uses state-of-the-art AI and computer vision technologies to send one unified message about the impact of our individual decisions on the planet. Four interactive installations can be physically explored on giant digital monoliths spread across cities, while a fifth collective artwork extends the experience online.
