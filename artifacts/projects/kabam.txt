title: @[Kabam] is a collection of game projects at game studio [Kabam Montreal](https://kabam.com/).

===

I worked at Kabam as a technical artist. My primary focus was Shadows of Valdora (cancelled), where I:

\- created tools for artists to test and validate assets

\- overhauled lighting

\- mediated between artists and developers regarding new technical features and constraints

\- monitored performance and followed up with optimization passes

\- dealt with graphics-related bugs across the production

\- set up and managed cutscene systems

\- assisted graphics programmers with new shaders and graphics pipeline changes

\- integrated 2D and 3D assets

\- produced and integrated VFX

\- managed and wrote documentation

![Screenshot of a 3D video game viewed from top down with three characters fighting a machine.](kabam/2.png)
![3D video game city by a river viewed from the top down.](kabam/3.png)

During downtime, I also helped the Shop Titans (shipped & live ops) team with similar tasks.

![Illustration of a heroic character holding a sword and a giant pink monster with two heads towering over him, both floating on flying rocks.](kabam/4.png)

After Shadows of Valdora's cancellation, I focused on look dev (with an emphasis on researching and prototyping stylized shading and character customization systems), rendering techniques, and prototyping new art tech for an unannounced title.
