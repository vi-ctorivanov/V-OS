![Photograph of 2 people at a museum observing a spherical projection of zoomed-in insects.](dpt/8.jpg)

During my time at Dpt, I worked on projects for clients and organizations like Meta, Felix & Paul, Jeld-Wen, Mandai Wildlife Reserve, and Calgary Stampede.

![Blue corridor with reflective screens on each side and a projected array of particles on the floor.](dpt/17.jpg)
![Astronaut at night on a desert plane with a rover near them.](dpt/5.jpg)

My major project was leading the technical pipeline for Space Explorers, an Unreal VR production for Felix & Paul. I authored master shaders for environmental elements like the ISS and various VFX, which meant producing low-cost materials on a mobile render pipeline that maintained the aesthetic targets for a major production. I also produced VFX for things like navigation and UX feedback, cutscenes, tutorials, XR transitions between in-game and passthrough content, and other stylized elements in the title's scenes.

![First person view of a miniature model of the ISS floating in a living room with a cosmic arm reaching towards it.](dpt/7.png)
![Large room with a globe in its center around which are gathered a dozen people, the ceiling curving inwards at the center.](dpt/10.jpg)

During that time, I also worked directly with the client, understanding the project's requirements, developing artistic concepts, and mapping those ideas to concrete designs. I also managed two other artists (technical and VFX) to help them grow their technical expertise in their respective areas of work.

![Dark room with statues of rock-embedded dinosaurs and a person standing in front of a large screen showing the face of a dinosaur.](dpt/12.jpg)
![Bright orange room with small dunes as the floor, each wall is a mirror, two people conversing and smiling in it.](dpt/13.jpg)

Beyond Space Explorers, I contributed to a large scale multi-installation exhibit (87 interactive installations!) at Mandai Wildlife Reserve in Singapore, where I made particle systems and shaders in Unity for environments and UX feedback.

I built character rigs for an interactive exhibit for Calgary Stampede and a web AR project for Jeld-Wen, where I also worked on Maya rigging tools for automating control rigs and helper bones.

Between major projects, I optimized topology and UVs across assets for various other productions, and I even got to work with some really fun and... *promise you won't tell anyone*... ***unreleased hardware***.
