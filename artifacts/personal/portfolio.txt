I'm Victor Ivanov [vi], and I'm the creator of this space, currently residing in Montreal. I've worked as a developer, artist, and technical artist for games, publicity, web, VR & AR experiences, and art installations.

I design, develop, and produce static and interactive media, focusing to create *unique, innovative, aesthetically powerful, polished, and imaginative* experiences.

In my %[new Date().getFullYear() - 2020;] years of work, I've put my art and programming skills to use across the realm of digital media for clients and studios like Meta, Felix & Paul, Dpt, Kabam, Iregular, and Desjardins, tackling challenges that balance technical expertise and artistic expression.

My experience covers:

\- Offline and realtime graphics for games and film
\- Full pipeline 3D art production and optimization
\- Shaders, realtime VFX, physics simulations, materials, and post processing
\- Procedural generation systems, art production pipeline design, and tool development
\- Development of full stack websites and realtime 3D web applications
\- Teaching all of the above

I feel most motivated and fulfilled when I'm learning new things, solving problems I'm not familiar with, and supporting other artists. I'm always looking for new opportunities to create something original and innovative, and to solve unique problems with unique solutions. If you think I can help with your project, regardless of size, send me an email at [vi@v-os.ca](mailto:vi@v-os.ca)!

I also keep my traditional art skillsets sharp by producing a lot of personal work, found on the @[home] page. I craft stories and art often focused on alternate realities and fictional worlds where technology is used as a narrative catalyst to explore existential questions.
